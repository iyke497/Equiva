Strengthening systems through evidence and partnership

To strengthen health and social systems through evidence, innovation, and partnerships that improve access, quality, and outcomes, particularly for women, children, and underserved communities.

Our Vision

Health, dignity, and opportunity for everyone

A world where health, education, dignity, and opportunity are accessible to all, regardless of gender, income, or geography.

We believe that true development begins with fairness, ensuring every person has equal access to the services, opportunities, and support that allow individuals and communities to thrive.

01
Universal access to quality healthcare and education
02
Systems that protect the vulnerable and empower women
03
Data‑driven governance that serves every community
04
Sustainable systems that endure beyond individual projects
05
A future where equity in life is a reality, not an aspiration
Our Values

What we stand for

Rooted in the meaning of our name, Equiva = Equity + Value, we champion fairness, wellbeing, and empowerment across every programme.

Aims & Objectives

What we're here to do

Every engagement is guided by clear, measurable goals aligned to the communities we serve.

Evidence‑Driven Decision‑Making

Strengthen routine information systems, improve data quality, and expand the use of analytics, dashboards, and insights that support effective policies, planning, and resource allocation.

Strengthen Resilient Systems

Improve service delivery, governance, quality of care, and workforce capacity so that communities, especially women and vulnerable groups, can access equitable, high-quality health and social services.

Drive Policy Change

Influence policy reforms, promote accountability, and advocate for improved health, education, nutrition, and gender outcomes that reflect community needs and strengthen equity.

Integrate Gender Equality

Identify and address gender barriers, promote women's leadership, and ensure all interventions are gender‑responsive and advance fairness and dignity for women and girls.

Improve PFM & Health Financing

Support governments in better budgeting, expenditure tracking, fiscal analysis, and strategic purchasing that enhance efficiency, transparency, and progress toward Universal Health Coverage.

Expand Access to Essential Services

Ensure health, nutrition, and education commodities and services are accessible, affordable, and high‑quality by connecting governments, manufacturers, and communities.

Resource Mobilisation

Receive and manage grants, donations, and other assistance from governments, development partners, and individuals in support of the organisation's mission and programmes.

Supportive & Enabling Functions

Undertake any lawful activities, initiatives, or actions that are incidental, necessary, or conducive to achieving the organisation's stated objectives and ensuring its effective mandate.

Why Equiva

Because everyone deserves to live with dignity, health, and value

Our name itself reflects our commitment: Equiva = Equity + Value, a promise to promote fairness, opportunity, and thriving communities. We operate at the intersection of health systems strengthening, data‑driven decision‑making, public financial management, gender equality, health financing, and strategic advocacy.

Through these integrated approaches, we build resilient, inclusive, and sustainable systems that deliver lasting impact. We bring together evidence‑based solutions, multi‑sectoral expertise, strong partnerships, a deep commitment to gender equality, and a people‑first approach, and translate these into real‑world impacts.

01
Evidence‑Based

Every programme backed by rigorous analysis and real‑world data.

02
Community First

The needs of the people we serve always come first.

03
Open Collaboration

Full transparency into our methods, funding, and outcomes.
