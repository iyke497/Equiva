Who We Are

Weaving together
passion and impact

Equiva brings evidence‑driven strategy and community‑centered design to organizations working to create lasting social change.

Vision

A world where every community has the tools, data, and partnerships to shape its own future — with equity at the center of every decision.

Mission

To strengthen systems, build capacity, and unlock data‑driven insights so that NGOs, governments, and communities can deliver measurable, lasting results.

Aims & Objectives

What we're here to do

Every engagement is guided by clear, measurable goals aligned to the communities we serve.

Strengthen Systems

Build resilient health, education, and financial systems that can withstand shocks and scale with demand.

Advance Equity

Integrate gender mainstreaming and inclusive design into every program, ensuring no community is left behind.

Unlock Data

Equip partners with analytics, dashboards, and monitoring frameworks that turn raw data into actionable strategy.

Why Equiva

Not your typical consultancy

Equiva was founded on a simple premise: exceptional social impact shouldn't be limited to large institutions. Our name — derived from equilibrium — reflects our approach: balancing deep analytical rigor with genuine community partnership. We don't parachute in with one‑size‑fits‑all frameworks. We embed with the communities we serve, learn their context, and co‑design solutions that stick.

Our team brings decades of combined experience across public health, financial management, data science, and policy advocacy. We've worked in over 30 countries, and we bring that global perspective to every local challenge.
