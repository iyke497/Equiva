We are a charitable NGO dedicated to advancing equity in health, education, and opportunity — especially for women and vulnerable communities.

Learn More
OUR VISION

A world where health, education, dignity, and opportunity are accessible to all, regardless of gender, income, or geography.

OUR MISSION

To strengthen health and social systems through evidence, innovation, and partnerships that improve access, quality, and outcomes — particularly for women, children, and underserved communities.

A What We Do

🌍 Health Systems

We strengthen governance, service delivery, and quality improvement to build resilient, people‑centered health systems.

📊 Data Analytics

Transforming data into evidence for policy and practice — dashboards, predictive models, and data quality assessments.

💸 Public Financial Management

Better budgeting, expenditure tracking, and fiscal analysis to ensure every dollar works harder for health and education.

⚕️ Health Financing

Designing equitable financing reforms that expand coverage and reduce financial hardship.

🛒 Market Shaping

Ensuring essential medicines and nutrition products are accessible, affordable, and high‑quality.

♀️ Gender Mainstreaming

Integrating gender equality into policies and programs — addressing structural barriers for women and girls.

👶🏽 MNCH

Ensuring every woman, newborn, and child survives and thrives through safe pregnancy care and community services.

🍎 Nutrition

Tackling malnutrition through food systems strengthening and maternal‑child feeding programs.

📚 Education

Strengthening inclusive, equitable education systems through teacher capacity building and data‑driven reforms.

📣 Advocacy & Policy

Amplifying community voices and influencing policy for accountable, people‑centered health and education.
